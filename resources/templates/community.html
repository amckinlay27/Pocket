Our Communities

One of the things that we love to do at Pocket is to give back to our local communities. These are just a few ways we give back to our communities in which we operate in.

Compile Local Initiative

This initiative was started by Pocket to expose more communities to the world of programming and all that it offers. We team up with business in the local community and partner them up with individuals with backgrounds in programming. The events are open to the community as we hope to spark new projects and ideas that use the power of programming to compile solutions for local needs.

The Arts

We provide funding to various institutions for funding art programs and provide scholarships to individuals pursuing the field. For centuries, the arts have been a way to capture history and show the cultural changes that unfold. Art also can showcase some amazing things, like the photos on our site. The phots were mostly taken locally around main offices.

Local Organizations

We donate not only our money, but we also donate our time to various local organizations in the communities we operate in. A strong bonded community is a prosperous one. Giving back is the least we can do for the communities that have trusted us as their bank. It’s a privilege, and it’s a privilege we try to pay forward.
